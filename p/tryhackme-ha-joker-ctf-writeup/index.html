1.7. Zip Cracking

┌──(kali㉿kali)-[/tmp]
└─$ unzip backup.zip
Archive:  backup.zip
[backup.zip] db/cmsdb.sql password:

Nothing is obvious as to what could be the password. I’ll use john.

Wait how?

  1. Using zip2john, we can get the hash: zip2john backup.zip > hash
  2. Then, do john hash and there you go!
